ABOUT US


"A History of Forward Thinking"


The Irish School has been an esteemed establishment for 40+ years, founded in 1982 by Marian Phelan. 


The Irish name in The Irish School Cairo is derived from the founder's home country.


Our Nursery has welcomed parents from 62 Nationalities since its founding. 


We welcome you all, and we look forward to being your child’s second home.

Subject Line: Zoom Meeting Request

PLEASE INCLUDE THE FOLLOWING INFO WHEN EMAILING US TO BETTER ASSIST YOU


  1. A phone number so we may call you if necessary

  2. Are you seeking French or English Department?

  3. Your Child/Children Name & Date of Birth?

  4. Inform us if your child has attended Nursery before.

  5. The intended start date for your child i.e the month and year you would like your child to start attending our Nursery.

  6. If you are moving to Egypt, please share with us your arrival date. 


Please note: we will organize an in person tour with you upon your arrival if you are registering from abroad so you may confirm that our Nursery is what you expected.

COMMONLY ASKED QUESTIONS


What are the Fees?

To obtain the fees you must book an appointment. 


Do you provide transportation for children to go to school?

No, we do not. 


Can we set up a Zoom? 

Yes, we can schedule a Zoom Tour with you, please see above.


Do I have to book an appointment to register my child? 

Yes. 


Do you offer an after-school problem? 

No. Last time to pickup your child is 4pm. 


Do we offer part time?

Yes.


Do we advise Part Time?

We don’t usually recommend it as we feel our children benefit strongly from routine and in result, thrive.


Are meals provided by the school?

No. 


Do we have a Summer Program?

Yes.  All Summer we are open.


Payment Types Accepted?

Cash Payments. As we are located in Egypt, please be aware that cash payments are customary and is the preferred method of payment. 


Do we take children without any basic foundation in English & French?

Yes. We welcome all levels.
